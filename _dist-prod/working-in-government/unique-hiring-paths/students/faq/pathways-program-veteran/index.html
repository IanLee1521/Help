Does veterans’ preference apply to the Pathways Programs jobs?

If you qualify for veterans’ preference, upload your DD-214, Certificate of Release or Discharge from Active Duty and, if applicable, SF 15, Application for 10-Point Veterans’ Preference. You can also attach your DD-214 or SF 15 to any resume you distribute at a Pathways hiring event.

Learn how to upload documents.

If you’re a veteran, visit http://www.fedshirevets.gov, email fedshirevets@opm.gov or call (202) 606-5090 for Federal hiring assistance.
